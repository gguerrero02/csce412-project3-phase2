How I Give Back

SPIES

The primary way in which I currently get to give back to the Bryan/College Station community is through SPIES (Sophomores Progressing in Excellence and Sustainability). In this organization I act as an assistant director of the public relations committe. As a whole, the organization strives to give sophomores a sense of community and a chance to learn and promote sustainable practices in their everyday lives. Through park clean-ups, fundraisers, and service events, SPIES provides plenty of opporunities to give back!
