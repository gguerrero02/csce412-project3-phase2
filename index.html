A little bit about me.

I am currently a 4th year Senior Computer Science major with a minor in Statistics and hopefully a minor in Game Design if I can get into the classes I need next semester!

Why this class?

I am very passionate about design and organization. Whether I'm getting people together at my house or coming up with new DIY projects, I'm always thinking of fun and inclusive ways for people to interact with my ideas, which is a mindset that I think will translate pretty well to this class.

How do I think this class will impact my career?

After college, I hope to enter the field of UI/UX design. From our short time in class already I have been looking for ways to make my UI designs more accessible and user-friendly. I hope that after this class is over, I'll have a solid framework and toolset for creating not only aesthetically pleasing user interfaces, but ones that include all users.

What courses have I taken and how do they relate to this course?

Last semester I took CSCE 315: Programming Studio. Over the entire course, my team and I were tasked with creating a point of sale system from scratch with front-end and back-end access. This not only tested my ability to create a good-looking and accessible user interface, but also my team building and inter-personal skills. I think both of these aspects will be vital for getting the most out of this course. I also took CSCE 412: Cloud Computing over the summer. One of the parts that was required for each project was documentation that demonstrated the implementation of the project as well as how the user should interact with the final project. This challenged me to go back through my work and try to view it from a new user's perspective, which is a process that I think will be helpful for this course as well.
