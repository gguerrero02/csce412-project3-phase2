Howdy!

My name is Garrett Guerrero. I am a computer science major at Texas A&M University. I consider myself a very well-rounded individual, so I bring a wide range of skills to the table and am always open to mastering new ones. First and foremost, I have a deep love and appreciation for the STEM field - I find the endless stream of problems being solved every day with new ideas and technological advances absolutely inspiring. However, STEM isn’t my only passion. The arts play a huge role in my daily life. Music, art, and design act as a driving force for me to not only deliver functional solutions to problems, but beautiful and expressive solutions as well. Expression is such an important part of who we are as humans, so why should we limit ourselves to bare-bones solutions? As I have made my way through college and participated in group labs, projects, and presentations, I have fostered my love of people. I believe in the power of building up those around you, that making strong interpersonal connections can lead to more innovation, creativity, and expression. I have also had the opportunity to act as an assistant director for a sustainability-based social organization on campus called SPIES (Sophomores Progressing in Excellence and Sustainability). I am currently helping lead the public relations committee, so not only do I get to nurture my creative side by designing merchandise and infographics, but I get to witness those that I lead grow closer together as friends. I hope whatever teams I lead in the future will grow together in the same way. Through my fascination with STEM, love of art, and desire to become the best leader and team-member that I can be. I believe my wide skill set also allows me to approach ideas with unique perspectives, so I think I make a great candidate for any team-based work environment!
